About Us

the improv company brings the art of improvisation to the workforce. Having worked in corporate cultures for over a decade, we know there is a lot of room for mixing things up. Collaboration, spontaneity, creativity, and support are the skills we build upon through our workshops. Our backgrounds are in advertising, marketing, public relations and technology. And, of course, performance and improvisational theatre. We’ve specialized in pitch coaching, creative thinking and problem solving. Thus, we are the perfect crew to hire for your next corporate offsite, meeting or event.

Hire us

We focus on corporate events and customize every session based on your needs. Everything we do is through the lens of “how can we bring these skills back to our everyday work to improve productivity, energy levels and engagement?” Our sessions tend to include:

  • Practicing the basics of improvisation, such as “yes, and” and “bring a brick, not a building”
  • Building on one another and exploring characters
  • Spontaneously creating short scenes
  • Creating environments of trust and team building
